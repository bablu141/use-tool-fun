Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

Our tool supports JPG, PNG, and WebP formats for both input and output.

Is there a file size limit?

You can convert images up to 10MB in size. For larger files, consider using professional software.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens in your browser. We don't upload or store your images anywhere.

Which format should I choose?

JPEG is best for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency, and WebP for modern web use with good compression.
